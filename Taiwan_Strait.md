## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Taiwan Strait is a 180-kilometer-wide (110 mi; 97 nmi) strait 
> separating the island of Taiwan and the Asian continent. 
> 
> The strait is part of the South China Sea and connects to the East China Sea to the north. 
> The narrowest part is 130 km (81 mi; 70 nmi) wide.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan%20Strait)
